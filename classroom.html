Instructions for accessing your Classroom

Welcome, students, to the Blockchain Transaction and Wallet Security class! We are excited to embark on this educational journey together. Before we begin, let's go over some instructions on how to access the class and make the most of our learning experience.

  • 1. Online Platform: Our class will be conducted through our user-friendly online learning platform. To access the class, kindly use the links below. If you haven't created an account yet, you can easily sign up for free on the platform.
  • 2. Class Schedule: Our class will take place every Sunday from 7PM to 9PM GMT+1 . Please ensure that you adjust the class timing to your local time zone to join the sessions promptly. We kindly request you to be punctual and ready to engage in the learning activities.
  • 3. Live Sessions: The class will consist of live sessions where our instructors will deliver lectures, conduct discussions, and address any questions or concerns you may have. These sessions offer an interactive learning experience, and we encourage active participation from all students.
  • 4. Course Materials: In addition to live sessions, you will have access to a wealth of course materials, including lecture notes, slides, readings, and supplementary resources. These materials will be available on the online platform for you to download and reference at your convenience.
  • 5. Communication and Support: Throughout the course, we value your engagement and encourage you to actively participate in discussions and ask questions. You can communicate with your instructors and fellow classmates through the online platform's discussion forums and messaging system.
